Pins!
- Pins now allow you to pin mods to the top of the list
and play them later (they are above favorites).
- This is in order for you to make a mod that you want to play,
but do not feel like marking it as a favorite.

How To Pin A Mod:
- To pin a mod, click the heart icon to the right of
the mod's cover or to the left of the mod's name.
- To unpin, click on the pin.
- You may also drag the pin into the cover to pin it,
or, drag the pin out of the cover to unpin it.
