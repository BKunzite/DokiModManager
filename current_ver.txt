- Username
You might notice that with this update,
the name on the home screen is different from
what it was before. This change was done
to support the shift to macOS cleaner whenever
I get the chance to add it.
It now uses the name of the computer instead
of the name of your user folder.
If you do not like it, you can now click on
the name to change it.

- Files For Mods
There are two ways to view files for mods:

1. Before you launch the mod:
    You can click on the file icon to the right of the
    play button to view the files
    of the mod in Explorer.

2. After you launch the mod:
    Focus back onto the mod manager, there
    should be a pill-shaped button that says "Files".
    Click on it to view the files of the mod in Explorer.
